About this website

Our app aims to create a platform where Chinese students and lovers of Chinese cuisine can explore, discuss, and share their thoughts on nearby Chinese restaurants. Users will have access to a list of nearby Chinese restaurants, which can be sorted by category from best to worst. The app also allows users to view the exact locations of these restaurants directly within the platform. By creating an account, users can leave ratings and reviews for their favorite Chinese restaurants and browse feedback shared by others. Chinese restaurant owners can showcase their establishments on the platform by uploading details, including photos and descriptions, and gain insights into customer opinions about their restaurants.
